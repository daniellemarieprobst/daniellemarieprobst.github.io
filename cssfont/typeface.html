GALAGA: THE TYPEFACE OF THE UNIVERSE

Galaga is a unique style typeface that that based on geometric shapes and is unified by small dots that accent each letter. The face looks like something straight out of outerspace--it is truly the typeface of the universe. Perfect for logos and header text, Galaga is great for just about every Sci-fi, space themed anything--if you want to be a bit more creative than Futura.
